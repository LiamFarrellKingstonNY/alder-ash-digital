An AI-powered photography workflow tool that automates the most time-consuming parts of post-shoot processing — culling, tagging, album building, and client delivery.

The Challenge

Solo photographers were spending 30–40% of their working hours on post-processing busywork — tasks that generate no additional revenue but eat into booking capacity and creative time. The goal was to eliminate that overhead entirely using AI.

What We Built

A custom AI pipeline that connects directly to a photographer's Lightroom catalog. It runs automated culling (rejecting blurry, duplicate, and poorly-composed shots), applies smart keyword tags for searchability, and generates shareable client galleries with one click.

Website & Booking Engine

The Willowbrook Inn

A boutique hotel website with a direct booking engine, room galleries, curated local experiences, and seasonal packages — designed for a historic 12-room inn in Hudson, NY.

12
Unique room listings
Direct
Booking engine
Curated
Local experiences

The Challenge

A historic inn was losing bookings to OTAs like Booking.com and Airbnb, paying 15–20% commissions on every reservation. They needed a site that could compete visually with those platforms while driving direct bookings — plus a way to showcase each uniquely designed room and the local experiences that make Hudson special.

What We Built

An editorial-style website with a built-in booking engine featuring date selection and availability checking. Each of the 12 rooms has its own detailed listing with galleries, amenities, and pricing. A curated experiences section offers local itineraries, and seasonal packages encourage longer stays and direct bookings over third-party platforms.
